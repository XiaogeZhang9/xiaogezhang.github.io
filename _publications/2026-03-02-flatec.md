FLaTEC presents a frequency-aware LiDAR point cloud compression framework that projects voxel features into compact triplanes and disentangles low- and high-frequency components for flexible bitrate allocation. With progressive frequency modulation and lightweight local refinement, it preserves fine structural details while enabling real-time compression and achieving state-of-the-art rate-distortion performance on SemanticKITTI and Ford benchmarks.
